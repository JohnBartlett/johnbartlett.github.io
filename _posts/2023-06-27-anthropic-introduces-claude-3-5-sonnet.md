The new model brings several significant enhancements:

1. **Reduced Hallucinations**: Claude 3.5 Sonnet shows a marked decrease in fabricated or inaccurate information.

4. **Consistency**: Claude 3.5 Sonnet maintains higher consistency across repeated queries.
